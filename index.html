Privacy Policy for Sages

Last Updated: April 2026
Welcome to Sages. Your privacy is a cornerstone of our Covenant. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our Council of Great Minds.

1. The Controller

Sages is operated by an individual developer. For any inquiries regarding your data or this policy, please contact us at:

sages.app.support@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with the Sages experience:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address provided during registration (handled via Supabase).
  • Interaction Data: The text of your "Dialogues" (chat history) with the AI Sages is stored so you can resume your conversations across sessions.
  • Payment Data: In the future, transactions will be handled exclusively by Apple App Store and Google Play Store billing systems. We do not store credit card details on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Authenticate your access to the Council.
  • Maintain your personal "Council History" and chat continuity.
  • Future AI Development: We may use anonymized interaction data to improve the "wisdom" and accuracy of our models. If we do this, personal identifiers (like your name or email) will be stripped from the data.

4. Third-Party Sentries

To bring the Great Minds to life, we share the text of your prompts (but not your name or email) with OpenAI or Google Gemini. Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy standards. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools at this time.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active.
  • Deletion: You have the right to depart the Council at any time. You can initiate a permanent account deletion within the App Settings. Upon deletion, your email, name, and all chat history will be wiped from our primary database.

6. Children’s Privacy

Sages is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age. If we discover such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

7. Changes to the Covenant

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app.
